Your Journey

Four simple steps, one contact person

  1. Send your reports

    Share your medical reports and complaints via WhatsApp or the form below. English, German or Turkish — as you prefer.

  2. Remote evaluation & plan

    The relevant specialist reviews your file. You receive a written evaluation and a proposed treatment plan; pricing is shared after this medical evaluation.

  3. Treatment in Istanbul

    Your coordinator schedules all appointments back-to-back so consultation, tests and imaging fit into the shortest possible stay.

  4. Follow-up at home

    You leave with your full file in English. Follow-up questions and check-in calls continue remotely after you return home.
